Sneak A Peek At Our Speciality:

Dosa:


A dosa is a thin batter-based pancake originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice. Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt, then fermented.

Hakka Noodles:

boiled noodles, stir fried with sauces and vegetables or meats

We provide veg, egg, chicken and mixed hakka noodles

Biryani:


Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Shahi Paneer:


Shahi paneer is a preparation of paneer, native to the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a thick gravy of cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.
